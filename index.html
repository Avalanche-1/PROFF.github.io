About Us

Welcome to Aseda Ben Hair Salon, where excellent customer service is our top priority.
Our team of talented and educated stylists and technicians are committed to providing exceptional hair services using state-of-the-art equipment.
Established in 2007 and located in Beauty Lane, Accra, our salon has become more than just a place for hair care;
it’s a warm and welcoming experience that will transform the way you look after your hair.
With a newly remodeled space in 2024, featuring modern amenities, we strive to offer you the best overall experience.
Let our innovative hair designers, stylists, and colorists create a new look for you.
We can’t wait to serve you! .
